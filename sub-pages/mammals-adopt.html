Mammals

While all effort is made to keep this section updated as changes happen at the rescue, this gallery list may not represent our complete list of adoptable mammals as we may receive or adopt out animals before being able to make updates. Please check back frequently for any changes, or contact us with questions!

Please note: Adoption fees that are a single price or that are listed first are for the mammal only. A secondary price may be included in the description if there is an enclosure available to take home, and that price reflects both the animal AND the enclosure. We DO NOT guarantee the age, sex or morph of any animal listed. Information is given to the best of our ability based on animal history given by the person surrendering it. Some animals come to us with no known history (e.g.: animals from law enforcement confiscations, shelter partners, or anonymous surrenders).

Submission of any adoption application does NOT guarantee approval. We reserve the right to deny applications due to strict placement policies for all animals at our rescue.
