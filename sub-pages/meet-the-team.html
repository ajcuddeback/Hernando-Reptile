Dan Frisher

Director of Veterinary Care

Dan has been working with animals since she was 13 by starting at her hometown zoo, then continued at a wildlife rehabilitation hospital as well as a big cat sanctuary, all before stepping into the veterinary world. She graduated from FSU in 2014 and has 8 years of vet tech experience from general practice to emergency/critical care. For the last 2 years she has been focused on exotic animal medicine. She has extensive knowledge on proper husbandry/dietary needs for exotic animals and loves to share that knowledge with others.

Dan recently moved to the Tampa bay area with her boyfriend and all of their animals. She heard the call for volunteers, joined up and became immediately active in all aspects of the rescue, wherever she could help. Most importantly, Dan has been instrumental in providing the TLC needed for some very sick intakes, saving the lives of several neglected and special needs reptiles. She is excited to help the rescue expand and make sure the animals receive medical care and excellent nutrition during their stay and we are thrilled to have her in such an important role.
