Birds

While all effort is made to keep this section updated as changes happen at the rescue, this gallery list may not represent our complete list of adoptable reptiles and amphibians as we may receive or adopt out animals before being able to make updates. Please check back frequently for any changes, or contact us with questions!

lease note: We DO NOT guarantee the sex, age or temperament of any bird. Information is given to the best of our ability based on animal history given by the person surrendering it. Some animals come to us with no known history (e.g.: animals from law enforcement confiscations, shelter partners, or anonymous surrenders).

While every effort is made to include an appropriate cage for each bird adopted, we do not guarantee the availability of cages upon adoption and you may need to provide your own cage. Here are our minimum cage recommendations:

Parakeets, Cockatiels, Lovebirds, Quakers: 27”w x 24”d

Ringnecks, Conures, Pionus, Lories, Meyers, Senegals: 32”w X 23”d

African Greys, Small Cockatoos, Eclectus, Amazons, Small Macaws: 36”w x 28”d

Large Cockatoos and Macaws: 48”w x 36”d

Extra Large Cockatoos and Macaws: 64”w x 32”d or larger.

Make sure bar spacing is appropriate for your bird.

There is a non-refundable $25 bird adoption application fee, face-to-face interview, and a home visit required prior to application approval. Submission of an adoption application does NOT guarantee approval. We reserve the right to deny applications due to strict placement policies for all animals at our rescue. ​
