Contact Us

Please note: If you are contacting us to surrender an animal, be aware that we are not an emergency care facility. While we handle rehabilitation of non-native and invasive species on a daily basis, we simply do not have the funding available to be able to take your pet to the vet on an emergency basis. Please take your pet to the nearest animal hospital in the event of an emergency.

If you need to report a Florida native wildlife emergency, please visit Owl's Nest Sanctuary for Wildlife at: www.owlsnestsanctuaryforwildlife.com/ or call the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission at 888-404-FWCC (3922).
